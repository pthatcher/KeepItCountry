What's the next step

The City Council may vote on whether to rezone to R-1 at their next Council Meeting. The Mayor decides the agenda for the meeting and only has to post it 24 hours in advance. If he adds this to the agenda, come to the meeting. Prior to that, there is a public hearing on Thursday, October 9th. Come to that meeting.

You can check the latest meeting agenda at Millville City's website.

If the City Council approves the rezoning, citizens of Millville have 5 days to file a referendum to challenge that decision. Such a referendum needs approximately 500 signatures from registered voters living in Millville. If this step is necessary, help get signatures from registered voters living in Millville who support the referendum.
