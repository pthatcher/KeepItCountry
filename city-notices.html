Millville City Notices Meetings, Agenda, and Minutes

Millville City keeps notices at millvilleut.gov/ and meeting agendas and minutes at millvilleut.gov/city-government/meeting-agendas-and-minutes .
You can also find notices, agendas and minutes by doing the following:
  1. Go to utah.gov/pmn
  2. Find "Government Type" and choose "Municipality"
  3. Find "Entity" and choose "Millville"
  4. Find "Public Body" and choose "City Council" or "Planning Commission"
  5. Scroll down to a list of notices
You can also subscribe to get email notices of upcoming meetings by choosing one of the notices on clicking on "subscribe to public body". Or just go straight to utah.gov/pmn/...subscribeByEmailSection
