Darcy Ripplinger

Darcy is running for City Countil. Here is what she says on her website (vote4darcy.com):
"I believe the growth of the city to be the biggest issue in the next few years. Having served on the planning & zoning commission for the past 6.5 years, I feel prepared to guide the direction of growth, by following the city's general plan and motto 'Country Living at Its Finest.'' I would like to see development that does not choke out our animals and agriculture in place of cars and pavement."
